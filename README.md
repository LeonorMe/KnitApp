# KnitApp

KnitApp is the project workspace for **Malha**, a knitting and crochet companion app concept.

## Malha

Malha is planned as a calm, offline-first crafting companion that helps users:

- Track knitting and crochet projects
- Follow patterns step by step
- Manage yarn, tools, and material purchases
- Finish more projects with less stash chaos
- Use future AI assistance through **Aidi**, a quiet craft planning assistant

The product direction is built around one principle:

> Reward creation, not consumption.
